Welcome to Vine:
Is this your first time?

The Vine is a student group that meets on the campus of the University of Windsor to provide students with an opportunity to socialize, worship, & be challenged in their Christian faith.

Drop by anytime
for almost everyone

We meet every Saturday night from September to April for a church-like experience featuring great worship music, and relevant teaching. The services start at 6:30 PM and go until 7:30 PM so make sure to get there a few minutes before in order to find a seat. After the service you will have the opportunity to meet other students and visit our welcome table for more information and resources!

DIRECTIONS

Currently we are meeting on the first floor of Toldo Building in room 104. The location is subject to change and any changes will be noted on our home page and sent via text message to those who have subscribed for updates. See below for a campus map including where to park and where to enter Toldo Building for services.
